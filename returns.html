Returns, refund & exchanges

Hassle‑free resolutions

What is your return policy?

Most items can be returned within 7–15 days of delivery. Check the product page for category‑specific policies and eligibility.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refunds are typically completed within 5–7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.

Will I be charged for return shipping?

Return shipping is free for eligible items. Some categories may require a small fee, shown clearly during the return request.

What if I received a damaged or wrong item?

Contact a live agent or raise a request with photos. We’ll arrange a replacement or refund quickly and keep you informed.
