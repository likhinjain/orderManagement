Order status & tracking

Know where your order is, every step of the way

How long does it take to process an order?

Most orders process within 24–48 hours. During peak times, slight delays can occur. We’ll notify you of any changes proactively.

Why was my order cancelled?

Common reasons include payment failure, stock unavailability, or address issues. You’ll receive a notification detailing the cause and next steps.

My order shows delivered but I haven’t received it — what should I do?

Contact a live agent. We’ll investigate with the courier and resolve it quickly, including replacements or refunds when applicable.

Why hasn’t my tracking number updated yet?

Tracking can take up to 24 hours to update after dispatch. If it doesn’t refresh beyond that window, reach out to support for assistance.

Can I change the delivery address after placing an order?

Address changes are possible only before shipping. Contact live support to update your details as soon as possible.
