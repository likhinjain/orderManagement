Manage and review your purchases

How can I view my order history?

Log in and open “My Orders” to see all past and current orders in one place. Each entry shows items, dates, statuses, and links to invoices or returns.

Can I cancel my order after placing it?

Yes, if the order hasn’t been delivered yet. Go to Main Menu → Cancel → choose item → provide a reason → confirm. You’ll get a confirmation email instantly.

Is there a time limit for cancelling an order?

Cancellations are possible before processing for delivery. Once shipped, you can’t cancel, but you may initiate a return after the item arrives.

Can I cancel part of my order?

Yes. For multi‑item orders, cancel individual items before they ship. The rest of the order remains unaffected and will be delivered as scheduled.
